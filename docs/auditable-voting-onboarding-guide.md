# Auditable Voting — Onboarding Guide for Coordinator

## Who Is This For?

This guide is for someone who wants to **create a poll and invite people to vote**. You don't need to know anything about Nostr, Bitcoin, or cryptography. The website handles all the technical stuff.

## What You Need

- A phone, tablet, or computer with a web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Internet connection
- About 15 minutes

---

## Step 1: Open the Coordinator Page

Open this link in your browser:

**https://vote.orangesync.tech/simple-coordinator.html**

This is your **coordinator dashboard**. You are the person running the poll.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Identity

When you first open the page, it will create a **personal identity** for you automatically. This is like a username — it identifies you as the poll organizer.

You should see your identity (a long code starting with `npub1...`) displayed on screen.

**Important**: There may be an option to **lock** your identity with a password. If you set a password, remember it — you'll need it next time you open the page.

## Step 3: Create Your Questionnaire

Look for the **Questionnaire** section on the coordinator page. You can create questions like:

### Yes/No Questions
> "Should we have pizza for dinner?" — Voters pick Yes or No

### Multiple Choice Questions  
> "What should we do Saturday?" — Voters pick one or more options from a list you define

### Ranked Choice Questions
> "Rank these activities from most to least preferred: Beach, Museum, Park, Cinema"
> — Voters drag to rank all options

### Free Text Questions
> "Any other suggestions?" — Voters type their own answer

For each question, fill in:
- **The question text** (what you're asking)
- **The options** (for choice/ranked questions) — add as many as you like
- Whether the question is **required**

When you're happy with all questions, click **Publish** or **Open** to make the questionnaire active.

## Step 4: Invite Your Voters

Once your questionnaire is active, you need to invite people. For each person who should vote:

1. Look for the **"Add participant"** or **"Generate invite"** button
2. The system creates a **unique private link** for that person
3. **Send that link to the person** via WhatsApp, email, text message, or show them a QR code

Each person gets a **different link**. The link is their personal ballot — only they can use it.

**For 5 voters, you'll create 5 separate invite links and send each one to a different person.**

## Step 5: Monitor Voting

Back on your coordinator page, you can see:
- Who has been invited
- Who has opened their link (claimed their invite)
- Who has submitted their vote
- How many votes have been received

The page **refreshes automatically** — just leave it open.

## Step 6: Close the Poll

When everyone has voted (or when time is up), close the poll on your coordinator page. You'll see the final results.

## Step 7: View Results

Results show for each question:
- **Yes/No**: count and percentage for each
- **Multiple choice**: how many picked each option
- **Ranked choice**: aggregated rankings
- **Free text**: all written responses

# How to Vote (For Participants)

You received a link from the poll organizer. Here's what to do:

## Step 1: Open Your Link

Tap or click the link you received. It will open a voting page in your browser.

**Use the same browser every time** — your identity is stored in that browser.

## Step 2: Your Identity Is Ready

The link automatically sets up your identity. You don't need to do anything — just wait for the ballot to appear.

## Step 3: Answer the Questions

Fill in your answers:
- **Yes/No**: tap Yes or No
- **Multiple choice**: pick your option(s)
- **Ranked choice**: drag items to rank them (first = most preferred)
- **Free text**: type your answer

## Step 4: Submit Your Vote

When you've answered all questions, tap **Submit**.

That's it! You've voted. You should see a confirmation message.

**Note**: You can only vote once. If you open the same link again, you won't be able to vote a second time.

# Helping Someone Else Vote

If someone needs help (e.g., a family member who isn't comfortable with technology):

1. Open **their personal link** on **their device** (or on your device if they're with you)
2. The ballot appears automatically
3. Read the questions to them
4. Let them choose their answers
5. Press Submit for them
6. Confirm they see the "Vote submitted" message

**Important**: Each person must use their **own unique link**. Don't mix up the links!

---

# Troubleshooting

**"The page doesn't load"** — Check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page.

**"I don't see any questions"** — The poll organizer may not have published the questionnaire yet. Wait a moment and refresh.

**"It says I already voted"** — You (or someone) already submitted a vote from this link. Each link can only be used once.

**"I want to change my vote"** — Sorry, votes are final once submitted. This is by design to keep the poll fair.
